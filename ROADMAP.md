# DM NATIVE — Roadmap

A living view of where DM NATIVE is and where it's going. We build security-first and deliberately, so dates are intentionally soft — **star the repo** to get notified as items ship.

> Legend: ✅ done · 🟡 in progress · ⏳ planned

## Foundation (the hard part — largely done)

- ✅ Signal Protocol end-to-end encryption (X3DH + Double Ratchet)
- ✅ Zero-knowledge message relay on the global edge
- ✅ Authenticated key exchange with MITM rejection
- ✅ Anonymous rooms with per-thread aliases
- ✅ Voice notes, media, polls, location, reactions & replies
- ✅ Live edge translation across 20+ languages
- ✅ Encrypted on-device storage

## Now → Beta

- 🟡 Real-device hardening across Android & iOS
- 🟡 Voice & video calling polish
- 🟡 Push notifications in production
- ⏳ Closed alpha → private beta program

## Next

- ⏳ Encrypted, recoverable backups (client-side encrypted)
- ⏳ Multi-device linking (desktop & web)
- ⏳ Sealed sender / reduced metadata
- ⏳ Disappearing messages
- ⏳ App Store & Google Play releases

## Trust & Transparency

- ⏳ Independent third-party security audit (results published here)
- ⏳ Published threat model & cryptography whitepaper
- ⏳ Reproducible builds
